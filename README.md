# Combat
Tools made to help you win fights and automate crystal PvP.
AutoCev – Places blocks and crystals automatically to trap and blow up people from above.
BasicAnchorAura – Places and charges Respawn Anchors to blow them up fast.
HeliumCrystalAura – Attacks and places crystals as fast as possible without lagging your game.
MaceKill – Automates the timing to one-shot players using the new Mace weapon.
MaceTpAura – Teleports you slightly to hit players and kill try not use it with mace kill cuz it have a mace kill integrated.

# Legit and UHC
Features meant to look like normal gameplay or for playing on competitive UHC servers.

## MacePvP:
LegitTotem – Puts a totem in your hand automatically but at a normal, human speed so you don't get banned.
PearlCatch – Helps you throw or intercept Ender Pearls accurately.
StunLam – Messes with the enemy's movement to make them easy targets.

## UHC:
AutoShieldBreak – Hits enemies with an axe the moment they raise their shield to disable it.
ComboTap – Resets your sprint automatically every time you hit someone to deal more knockback and start a combo.
TriggerBot – Attacks automatically the exact millisecond your crosshair touches a player.

# Render
Changes how your game looks and feels.
CustomLoadingScreen – Gives you a custom background and look when your game is loading up.
ImpactFramesModule – Makes your screen flash or show a visual effect when you land a hit.
WeatherChanger – Changes the weather to always sunny, raining, or clear, but only for you.

# Utility
Everyday tools to automate boring tasks.
AutoEnchantTable – Automatically clicks the enchanting table until you get the perfect enchants.
DeathSounds – Plays your own custom music or sound files when you die or when you respawn.
FastBreak – Breaks blocks faster by skipping the normal vanilla delays.
HeliumRPC – Shows your friends on Discord that you are playing Minecraft with this addon.
PackUtilToggle – Turns packutil on or off.
SafeElytra – Deploys your elytra automatically before you hit the ground so you don't die from fall damage.
TrapAssist – Helps you quickly build obsidian boxes around your targets to trap them.
UltraGapple – Eats golden apples automatically when your health drops, timed perfectly so the server registers it.
UltraOffhand – Smart controller that chooses whether to hold a totem, a gapple, or a shield in your left hand based on your health.
UltraSurround – Instantly surrounds your feet with obsidian so people can't use crystals against you.
VaultHack – Interacts with Trial Vaults automatically to claim your loot instantly.

# Miscellaneous (Shit)
Autism – Random movements and weird chat actions just for fun.
FactSpammer – Automatically types random facts or jokes in the chat to annoy other players.

# Requirements and Installation
1. Make sure you have Fabric Loader installed for your Minecraft version.
2. Download Meteor Client and drop it into your mods folder.
3. Download the Fabric Api and drop it into your mods folder.
4. Put the Helium Addon file into the same mods folder.
5. Launch your game using a launcher like Prism Launcher.
